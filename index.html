Steel Drivers Fan Site

Bio

The Steel Drivers are a band that specializes in the sounds of bluegrass music. From Nashville, Tennessee, the Steel Drivers consists of:

  • Tammy Rogers, fiddle
  • Mike Fleming, bassist
  • Gary Nichols, vocals and guitarist
  • Brent Truitt, mandolin
  • Richard Bailey, piano

For many years, the Steel Drivers played at bluegrass festivals where they were a hit with fans and critics. In 2009, they received a Grammy nomination for Best Country Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals.

They would follow that up with two nominations in 2010, and a win in 2016 for Best Bluegrass Album.
